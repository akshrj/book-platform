## 1. Rendezvous with Rama by Arthur C. Clarke

An explicit exploration of architectural geometry inside a cylindrical spacecraft. The narrative velocity is completely driven by structural exploration, forensic data extraction, and physical exploration of the alien layout.

## 2. The Three-Body Problem by Cixin Liu

A comprehensive assessment of grand physics systems combined with structural orbital defense strategies. The plot momentum utilizes strict mechanical limits to push the bounds of cosmic survival.
